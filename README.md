## 🖥️ What is annexa?

annexa helps engineers and teams track and manage compliance with the ISO 27001 standard. This standard sets rules for managing information security. annexa makes the process simpler by organizing key compliance information and tools into a single app.

You will find:

- A compliance heatmap that shows how well you meet ISO 27001 controls.
- A risk register to identify and assess risks.
- Guides for controls and actions you can take.
- Features that support GDPR, NIST, PCI-DSS, SOC2, and other standards.
- Offline use, so your data stays private.

annexa does not need internet access or signups. You can use it alone or with your team.

## 📋 Using annexa

Here are the basic features to get you started.

### Compliance Heatmap

- The heatmap shows your progress in meeting ISO 27001 controls.
- Controls are marked in colors representing status: complete, in progress, or not started.
- Click on any control to see details and suggestions.

### Risk Register

- Add risks by typing their description and severity.
- Assign categories such as "Data Protection" or "Network Security."
- The app scores risks to help you prioritize.
- Update the status as you take action.

### Controls Guide

- Find detailed explanations of each control in the ISO 27001 standard.
- Follow steps recommended for compliance.
- The guide is easy to read and helps you plan your next steps.
